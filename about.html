About Diploma Notes

A student-built platform making polytechnic education accessible to everyone.

🎯

Our Mission

Diploma Notes was created to simplify access to quality study materials for polytechnic students. We believe that education should be free, accessible, and organized. Our platform brings together notes, practicals, video tutorials, and previous year questions—all in one place.

📚

Our Story

Started by students of Government Polytechnic Jamuniya Deeh Harakh Barabanki, this project began as a simple idea: "What if all our study materials were available in one organized website?" What started as notes sharing among classmates has now grown into a comprehensive platform serving students across multiple branches.

Every piece of content on this platform is reviewed with faculty guidance to ensure accuracy and alignment with the official syllabus and examination patterns. We're proud to be a student initiative that prioritizes quality and accessibility.

✨

What We Offer

📝 Comprehensive Notes

Unit-wise notes strictly aligned with official syllabus and examination patterns.

🔬 Practical Manuals

Step-by-step practical guides with virtual lab links for hands-on learning.

🎥 Video Tutorials

Curated video content from trusted sources to help you understand concepts better.

📄 Previous Year Questions

Year-wise PYQs to help you prepare effectively for examinations.
