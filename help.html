Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Diploma Notes is a free, student-built educational platform that provides comprehensive study materials for polytechnic students. We offer notes, practicals, video tutorials, and previous year questions (PYQs) for Electrical, Mechanical, and Chemical Engineering branches.

Yes! Diploma Notes is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or paywalls. We believe education should be accessible to everyone. All study materials, notes, and resources are available at no cost.

Content is contributed by polytechnic students and reviewed with faculty guidance to ensure accuracy. All study materials are aligned with the official syllabus and examination patterns. Contributors are properly credited for their work.

Using the Platform

1. Go to the homepage
2. Select your branch (Electrical, Mechanical, or Chemical)
3. Choose your semester
4. Click on the subject you need
5. Browse through Notes, Practicals, Videos, or PYQs tabs

Yes! All PDFs (notes, practicals, PYQs) can be downloaded for offline study. Simply click the "Download" button next to any material. You can also view them online using the "Open" button.

No account is required! You can access all study materials without signing up. However, your recently viewed subjects are saved in your browser for easy access next time you visit.

Content & Quality

Yes! All study materials are strictly aligned with the official polytechnic syllabus and examination patterns. Notes are unit-wise and cover all important topics as per the curriculum.

Contributing

Technical Issues

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. Check your internet connection
If the issue persists, please report it with details about your browser and device.

- Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser settings
- Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
- Try right-clicking the download button and selecting "Save Link As"
- Disable any download manager extensions temporarily
If the problem continues, contact us.
