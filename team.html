Our Development Philosophy

Every feature we build starts with a simple question: "Will this genuinely help a student learn better?" We don't add complexity for its own sake. Instead, we focus on creating intuitive, accessible tools that solve real problems. Our codebase reflects our values—clean, documented, and built to last.
